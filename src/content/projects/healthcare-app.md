## Overview
Redesigning the appointment booking flow to reduce drop-off and confusion.

## My Role
UX Researcher — user interviews, usability testing, synthesis

## Research Questions
- Where do users feel uncertain during booking?
- What information is missing before confirmation?

## Methods
- 5 usability tests (remote)
- Affinity mapping
- Journey mapping

## Key Insights
Users lacked confidence in appointment availability and doctor specialization.

## Outcome
Proposed clearer slot labeling and doctor profiles → reduced drop-off in prototype tests.
